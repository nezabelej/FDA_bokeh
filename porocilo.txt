OpenFDA is designed primarily for queries against our powerful search API.

API ima implementiran Elastic search, tudi poizvedbe so v Elastic Search sintaksi.
Če bi hoteli bazo imeti lokalno, bi morali za hitre poizvedbe prav tako implementirati Elastic Search.

Če bi hotla v MySQL: treba bi bilo na roke procesirat in na roke normalizirat, stringe na roke joinat...

To keep your downloaded data up to date, you need to re-download the data every time it is updated.
